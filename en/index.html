Profile

PRIDASK is a cyber-music duo formed by vocalist Aikapin and producer/DJ Tomoyuki Sakakida.
Blending electronic and game-music roots with a cyber-punk worldview, they combine 3DCG visuals and original game titles to create truly cross-media experiences.
Operating without any record label or management agency, they remain a fully independent unit—writing, recording, designing, and coding every work themselves.
