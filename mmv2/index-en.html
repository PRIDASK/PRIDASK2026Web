Story

New experience where music meets RPG.

Hachiware Town is alive with a festival.
But a calamity called “AKATSUKI” strikes without warning.
It amplifies the darkness in people’s hearts and erodes their reason.
Can you become the hero who saves this town?
An adventure where human drama intertwines with sound and story.

Immersive Live Show

At the climax, light, visuals, and sound come together for a deeply immersive live show.
A fresh game experience that envelops your senses.

System

Exploration × Quests

To calm the town’s disturbed hearts, you’ll need the power of sacred talismans.
Collect seven talismans and quell “AKATSUKI”!

Varied Locations

Stations, shopping streets, department stores, karaoke, cafés, eateries, shrines, hot springs—every visit brings new discoveries.

Evolving Residents

Dialogue and behavior shift with events and progress. Your choices gently move the town forward.

Characters

Aikapin

Vocalist of PRIDASK, the music unit performing at Hachiware Town’s festival. Seems to be troubled by the influence of “AKATSUKI.”

Sakakida

DJ of PRIDASK. At times acts as the player’s “DJ mentor,” lending a hand.

Stationmaster Tao

Stationmaster of Hachiware Station, where your story begins—a big brother figure who loves the town.

Lanlan

A lively station attendant—playful, free-spirited, and adored by all.

Roo

Lanlan’s twin brother. Gentle-natured and fond of Stationmaster Tao.
